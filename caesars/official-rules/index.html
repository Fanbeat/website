How to Play

The Promotion is a free (standard data usage and other carrier fees may apply) game for fans to play during any Entry Period. For each Entry Period, as directed, a minimum of twenty-two (22) skill-based questions during regulation game time and up to three (3) additional questions per overtime period will be posed and will be available in the App beginning at the start of the Entry Period and thereafter during the applicable Entry Period. For each question participants must submit an answer before the timer (typically 30-45 seconds) expires at the bottom of the screen. The Promotion will consist of random drawings ("Instant Win Prizing" and "Promotion Period Prizing") and a skill-based competition ("Contest") as follows:

  • Entry Period Instant Win Prizing (Drawing(s)): For each question answered during an Entry Period by an eligible participant without regard for whether the answer is correct, such eligible participant will receive one (1) entry in an Instant Win animated drawing(s) for the applicable Entry Period. For each Entry Period, one (1) potential winner of the Drawing will be selected by random drawing from among all eligible entries received during such Entry Period. FanBeat and Caesars, may elect, from time to time during the Promotion Period to conduct multiple Instant Win Drawings during an Entry Period. Subject to verification of eligibility and compliance with the terms of these Official Rules, the potential winners will be declared the official winners of the Instant Win Drawing (each, an "Instant Win Drawing Winner"). Odds of winning an Instant Win Drawing Prize depend on the total number of eligible Instant Win Drawing entries received during the applicable Entry Period.
  • Promotion Period Prizing (Drawing): At the end of the Promotion Period (listed specifically in the Promotion Period section above), all eligible participants that answered questions during the Promotion Period, without regard for whether the answers are correct, will receive one (1) entry in the Promotion Period Drawing for each question they answered. The participant will be entered into a Promotion Period Prize Drawing for the identified Promotion Period Prizes (identified below as the "Promotion Period Prizes") and subject to verification of eligibility and compliance with these Official Rules. The Promotion Period Prize Drawing process is executed as follows: each question answered by an eligible participant during the Promotion Period will be converted to a ticket and the Operator will randomly draw a winning ticket for each Promotional Period Prize out of the sum total of all eligible players. The Operator will conduct the Prize Drawing in order of highest ARV prize to lowest ARV prize. Participants are not eligible to win multiple Promotion Period Prizes. Those eligible participants whose names are drawn offline by the Operator will be declared official winners of the Promotion Period. Odds of winning a Promotion Period Prize depend on the total number of eligible Promotion Period Prize entries received and the number of total questions answered during the Promotion.
  • Entry Period Contest Prizing: For each question correctly answered during an Entry Period by an eligible participant, such eligible participant will receive the points specified for the correctly answered question. These points will accumulate for the Entry Period, identified in the game as Game Points, where Game Points is defined as the aggregate value of all points earned for questions answered correctly during the Entry Period and displayed on the Game Leaderboard in the App.
    • At the end of each Entry Period, the eligible participants that received the most points for such Entry Period will be potential winners and subject to verification of eligibility and compliance with these Official Rules, will be declared official winners of the Contest for that Entry Period (each, an "Contest Game Winner"). In the event of a tie for a specific Entry Period prize (each, a "Contest Game Prize"), the participant who had the highest score on the final countdown trivia question for the Entry Period will be considered the winner of the Contest Game Prize. In the event that the tied participants also had equal scores on the final countdown trivia question, the participant who had the highest score on the second to last countdown trivia question for the Entry Period will be deemed the Contest Game Winner. In the event that the tied participants continue to be tied, score comparisons on each subsequent countdown trivia questions will be compared until a winner is determined. In the event that the tied participants are still tied after all countdown trivia questions are compared, each participant will be awarded a Contest Game Prize.
    • During the Promotion Period, any eligible participant who answers all Entry Period questions correctly, when the total number of questions asked is no fewer than twenty-two (22) questions for an Entry Period, will be a potential Grand Prize winner ("Grand Prize Winner") subject to verification of eligibility and compliance with these Official Rules. In the event that multiple participants qualify as potential Grand Prize Winners for a single Entry Period, the qualifying potential Grand Prize Winners will receive an equal split of the Grand Prize (defined below) offered for the Entry Period.

Grand Prize

Any eligible participant that answers all Entry Period questions correctly during a single Entry Period, and when the total number of questions asked is no fewer than twenty-two (22), as determined by the Operator in its sole discretion, will win all or a portion of one million U.S. dollars (US $1,000,000) awarded in a lump sum as described below (the "Grand Prize").

In the event that more than one (1) eligible participant answers all Entry Period questions correctly during a single Entry Period, when the total number of questions asked is no fewer than twenty-two (22), and qualifies for the Grand Prize, the Grand Prize will be divided evenly among all qualifying participants for that Entry Period. Payments will be made to any Grand Prize Winner by the Operator's insurer.

In no event will more than two (2) total Grand Prizes be awarded for the entire aggregate Promotion Period. In the event that two (2) total Grand Prizes are awarded during the Promotion Period, the Promotion will continue through the end of the Promotion Period as scheduled with all other prizes available to win.

Before claiming the Grand Prize, a Grand Prize Winner will be required to provide an affidavit attesting to his or her eligibility and compliance with the Official Rules of the Promotion, along with any other written documentation or information required by the Operator's insurer.

Collegiate basketball tournament games and event dates and times are determined at the sole discretion of the organizer and are subject to change. Released Parties will not be responsible for weather conditions, Acts of God, acts of terrorism, civil disturbances, work stoppage or any other event outside their control that may cause the cancellation or postponement of the collegiate basketball tournament event or any games.

From time to time, Operator may void (cancel) a question that it determines in its sole discretion was either (a) incorrectly stated or answered, or (b) is not applicable based on the game play. In this situation the voided question will not count as an active question in determining participants' eligibility for the Grand Prize, but any participant who answered the voided question will still receive an entry into the applicable prize drawing(s) for answering the question. The voided question may be replaced by Operator in order to offer at least twenty-two (22) valid, non-voided questions. In all cases, a Grand Prize Winner is required to correctly answer all valid questions of an Entry Period, with a minimum of at least twenty-two (22) valid, non-voided questions in order to be eligible to win the Grand Prize.

In the event that an error is made by the Operator and fewer than twenty-two (22) valid questions are posted for an Entry Period, no participants will be eligible to win the Grand Prize for that Entry Period.
